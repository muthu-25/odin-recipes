Croissant Recipe

Description

Learn how to make delicious homemade croissants with this easy-to-follow recipe. These buttery, flaky pastries are perfect for breakfast or anytime you want to indulge in a delightful treat.

Making croissants from scratch may seem intimidating, but with our step-by-step instructions, you'll be amazed at how simple and rewarding the process can be. From creating the dough to the final golden bake, enjoy the journey of crafting these classic French pastries in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 1 cup warm milk (110°F/45°C)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm milk, sugar, and yeast mixture. Stir well.
  3. Add the flour and salt to the wet ingredients, and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth.
  5. Roll out the dough into a rectangle and place the chilled butter in the center. Fold the dough over the butter, sealing the edges.
  6. Roll out the dough again, then fold it into thirds like a letter. Repeat this process 2 more times.
  7. Chill the dough for at least 1 hour.
  8. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  9. Roll out the chilled dough, cut it into triangles, and roll each triangle into a croissant shape.
  10. Place the croissants on a baking sheet, brush with beaten egg, and bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  11. Allow the croissants to cool slightly before serving. Enjoy your homemade croissants!
