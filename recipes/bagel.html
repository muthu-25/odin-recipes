Bagel Recipe

Description

A bagel is a round bread with a hole in the middle, topped with seeds, often flavored with onion, sesame, poppy, or everything.

It is a staple in Jewish cuisine and is typically topped with an assortment of seeds, such as sesame, poppy, pumpkin, sunflower, or everything. Here is a simple recipe for homemade bagels:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bread flour
  • 1/4 cup vital wheat gluten
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons instant dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, vital wheat gluten, salt, sugar, and instant dry yeast.
  2. Gradually add the warm water and mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and let the dough rise for 30 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into 8 equal portions and shape each portion into a round ball.
  6. Flatten the balls slightly and let them rest for 10 minutes.
  7. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  8. On a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, place the bagels with enough space between them.
  9. Optionally, sprinkle the bagels with seeds or everything bagel seasoning.
  10. Bake the bagels for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
